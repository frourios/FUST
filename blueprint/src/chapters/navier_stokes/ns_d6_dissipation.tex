\section{\texorpdfstring{$N_6$}{N6} as \texorpdfstring{$D_\zeta$}{Dζ} AF-Channel Projection}

$N_6$ is the antisymmetric (AF) channel projection of the unified operator $D_\zeta$ at normalization power $r = 1$. For $\varphi = (1+\sqrt{5})/2$ and $\psi = (1-\sqrt{5})/2$:

\begin{definition}[$N_6$: $D_\zeta$ AF-Channel Projection]
\label{def:ns_D6}
\lean{FUST.N6}
\leanok
\[
N_6[f](z) = \frac{f(\varphi^3 z) - 3f(\varphi^2 z) + f(\varphi z) - f(\psi z) + 3f(\psi^2 z) - f(\psi^3 z)}{(\varphi - \psi)^5 z}
\]
\end{definition}

\section{Spectral Coefficients}
\label{sec:ns_dissipation_coeff}

The spectral coefficient of $D_\zeta$'s AF-channel for the $n$-th mode:

\begin{definition}[Spectral Coefficient $C_n$]
\label{def:ns_dissipation}
\lean{FUST.NavierStokes.dissipationCoeff}
\leanok
\[
C_n = \varphi^{3n} - 3\varphi^{2n} + \varphi^n - \psi^n + 3\psi^{2n} - \psi^{3n}
\]
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[Vanishing in Kernel]
\label{thm:ns_kernel_vanish}
\lean{FUST.NavierStokes.dissipationCoeff_zero, FUST.NavierStokes.dissipationCoeff_one, FUST.NavierStokes.dissipationCoeff_two}
\leanok
\[
C_0 = C_1 = C_2 = 0
\]
\end{theorem}

\begin{theorem}[Dissipation Outside Kernel]
\label{thm:ns_dissipation_outside}
\lean{FUST.NavierStokes.dissipation_positive_outside_kernel}
\leanok
\[
n \geq 3 \Rightarrow C_n^2 > 0
\]
\end{theorem}

The boundary $n = 3$ marks the first mode beyond the spatial DOF of $D_\zeta$'s 4D spacetime: $\ker(F_\zeta) = \mathrm{span}\{1, z, z^2\}$ captures spatial structure, while $n \geq 3$ modes carry dynamical (temporal) information and dissipate.
