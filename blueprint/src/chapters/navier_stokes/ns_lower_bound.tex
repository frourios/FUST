\section{Upper and Lower Bounds}
\label{sec:ns_bounds}

Upper bound on the AF-channel spectral coefficient:
\[
|C_n| \leq 10 \cdot \varphi^{3n}
\]

For proving global existence, the upper bound alone is insufficient; the \textbf{lower bound} is essential.

\begin{theorem}[Dissipation Lower Bound]
\label{thm:ns_lower_bound}
\lean{FUST.NavierStokes.dissipation_lower_bound}
\leanok
\[
n \geq 4 \Rightarrow C_n \geq \frac{1}{3} \varphi^{3n}
\]
\end{theorem}

\section{Proof Outline}
\label{sec:ns_lower_proof}

\begin{proof}[Proof outline]
\begin{enumerate}
\item Decomposition of the leading term:
\[
C_n = \varphi^{3n}(1 - 3\varphi^{-n} + \varphi^{-2n}) + (\psi\text{-terms})
\]

\item Lower bound on the leading factor for $n \geq 4$:
\[
1 - 3\varphi^{-n} + \varphi^{-2n} > \frac{1}{2}
\]

\item The $\psi$-terms decay exponentially since $|\psi| < 1$, and are negligible compared to $\varphi^{3n}$

\item Combining:
\[
C_n > \frac{1}{2}\varphi^{3n} - 5 > \frac{1}{3}\varphi^{3n}
\]
\end{enumerate}
\end{proof}

\section{Physical Meaning in \texorpdfstring{$D_\zeta$}{Dζ} 4D Spacetime}
\label{sec:ns_lower_physics}

The lower bound guarantees that in $D_\zeta$'s 4D spacetime ($I_4 = \mathrm{Fin}\,3 \oplus \mathrm{Fin}\,1$):
\begin{itemize}
\item Dissipation at modes beyond the 3 spatial DOF ($n \geq 3$) never vanishes
\item Energy cannot accumulate at high modes
\item Finite-time blowup is structurally impossible
\end{itemize}
