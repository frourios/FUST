\label{sec:pd_principles}

\begin{enumerate}
\item Binomial coefficients $C(n,k)$ from D-structure combinatorics
\item Kernel dimensions $\dim\ker(D_m)$
\item The golden ratio $\varphi$ and its algebraic properties ($\sqrt{5}$, Fibonacci/Lucas numbers)

No phenomenological fitting is involved.
